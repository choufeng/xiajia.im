# Summarizing & Action Items

> 日期：2026-06-21 · 场景：会议收尾、固化行动项

## 🎯 为什么要练"收尾"

会开完没结论、没人接活、没人记得截止日——等于没开。英语会议的收尾有一套固定话术，把模糊讨论变成可追踪的行动。

五种策略覆盖所有「该收场」的时刻：

| 策略 | 一句话用途 |
| --- | --- |
| Numbered recap | 编号复述，确保记全 |
| Assign owner | 明确谁负责，拒绝"大家一起" |
| Pin deadline | 钉死截止日，拒绝"尽快" |
| Parking lot | 搁置离题项，不丢也不跑题 |
| Confirm close | 确认无遗漏再散会 |

记住顺序：**复述 → 分派 → 钉日期 → 搁置 → 确认**。好的收尾让会后没人能说「我以为是你做」。

## 💡 五种收尾策略

### 1. Numbered recap — 编号复述要点

**何时用**：会议内容多，怕记漏。用编号一条条过——既显严谨，又让所有人核对。

**句式模板**：
- *Let me **recap** — [一], [二], and [三].*
- *To summarize, three decisions: [一], [二], [三].*
- *So we agreed on: first [一], second [二].*

> **例**：Let me recap — three decisions: the feature flag ships Tuesday, the rollback doc is due Thursday, and we skip the EU rollout.

### 2. Assign owner — 明确负责人

**何时用**：每件事必须有 owner。模糊的「大家一起做」=没人做。点名分派。

**句式模板**：
- *[名字] owns [任务].*
- *Who's taking the **action item** for [任务]?*
- *Let's assign an **owner** — [名字], can you take it?*

> **例**：Maria owns the rollback doc. (Good catch on asking who's the owner.)

### 3. Pin deadline — 钉死截止日

**何时用**：光有 owner 不够，还要有日期。把"尽快"逼成具体日子——否则永远拖。

**句式模板**：
- *The **due date** is [具体日期/时间].*
- *Can we commit to [具体日期]?*
- *When can you have this by? Let's pin a date.*

> **例**：The due date is end of day Thursday.

### 4. Parking lot — 搁置离题项

**何时用**：会中冒出重要但跑题的话题。不丢（记下来），但也不让它跑题——放进 parking lot。

**句式模板**：
- *Let's put that in the **parking lot** and **take it offline**.*
- *Good point — let's park it for a follow-up.*
- *That's worth its own discussion. Parking lot?*

> **例**：Let's put that in the parking lot and take it offline after this meeting.

### 5. Confirm close — 确认无遗漏再散

**何时用**：复述完、分派完，最后确认一句「还有别的吗」——把遗漏的最后机会给所有人。

**句式模板**：
- *Anything else, or are we good to close out?*
- *Did I miss anything?*
- *Last call before we wrap — any open items?*

> **例**：Anything else, or are we good to close out?

**A**: Let me **recap** before we wrap. Three decisions: the feature flag ships Tuesday, the rollback doc is due Thursday, and we skip the EU rollout for now.
<span style="font-size:0.85em;color:var(--vp-c-text-2)">A：收尾前复述。三个决定：特性开关周二上线，回滚文档周四截止，欧盟暂不上。</span>

**B**: Got it. And the **action item** for the rollback doc — who's the **owner**?
<span style="font-size:0.85em;color:var(--vp-c-text-2)">B：收到。那回滚文档的行动项——谁负责？</span>

**A**: Good catch. Maria owns the doc. The **due date** is end of day Thursday.
<span style="font-size:0.85em;color:var(--vp-c-text-2)">A：问得好。文档归 Maria。截止周四下班前。</span>
